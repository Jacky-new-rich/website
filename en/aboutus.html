NEW RICH DESIGN新力奇科技設計

We began as a design company in 2014, focus on custom services like hardware design on digital and analog circuits, firmware writing & debugging, and mechanism design in different markets. We committed to our client for providing a sensor that is easy to use and also has high accuracy. After we developed several versions, we launched "VI-TEX series" in 2018. VI-TEX is a multi-functional vibration sensor, which can provide real-time digital vibration signal to the users. With practical experience in various fields like vehicle monitoring or machine tool monitoring. All VI-TEX sensors are equipped with the algorithm by CRC32 and ECC to keep the high accuracy and data safety. The high-end version is more capable of edge computing integration, making your data management be easier than before.

Core Technology

  • Digital Data Processing

    We complete the signal processing and vibration value calculation internally and output digital data. Make the signal be clearer and less susceptible to environmental interference.

  • Miniature Mechanism Design

    A huge system functions are integrated into a small shell. Make your vibration monitoring have fewer limitations when setting up and also can provide more detection items.

  • Algorithm development

    For different target, we can provide different analytic function to fit the usage situation of your equipment and trigger it.

Why we NEED
Vibration Monitoring

Vibration signal is one of the effective indicators to reflect equipment damage. For ensuring the safe mechanical operation process, if we can detect it effectively, we can maintain it in the right time and extend the life of the equipment and keep all operation is safe and accuracy.
