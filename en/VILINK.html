VI-LINK-TB RS485 Terminal Box

Collocated Sensors：All of the VI-TEX Series

    • The standard terminal box of VI-TEX series, support using line to directly connect to the PLC. It's total have 8 ports, including GND, Power, Out, In, Tx+, Tx-, Rx+ and Rx-.
    • Shell MaterialAluminum
    • Operating Temperature Range-40℃ ≦ T ≦ 85℃
    • Input10 Pin Shielded Connector
    • I/O Portup to 8 Port
    • DC Input12 ~ 24 V / 200mA
    • Weight60g

VI-LINK-USB 485 to USB Terminal Box

Collocated Sensors：All of the VI-TEX Series

    • Using virtual com port to transfer the data to your PC. The power is also provided by PC. It can help you to connect out VI Analyzer and view and save real-time data, or you can also adjust the trigger value or other request for research.
    • Shell MaterialAluminum
    • Operating Temperature Range-40℃ ≦ T ≦ 85℃
    • Input10 Pin Shielded Connector
    • OutputMicro USB
    • DC Input5 V / 500mA
    • Weight60g

Collocated Sensors：All of the VI-TEX Series

    • Support BT 5.0 communication directly to the computer.The maximum transmission distance can exceed 200 meters. It can also support NFC near-field induction quick pairing setting. The battery is built-in. Without additional power, it can run up to 8 hours.
    • Shell MaterialAluminum
    • Operating Temperature Range-40℃ ≦ T ≦ 85℃
    • Weight60g
