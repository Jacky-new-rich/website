Description

VI-TEX-A provide the function for single axis vibration detection. It does't limit to a spefic direction and can base on your request to installation and adjustment. Since VI-TEX-A has a higher operating temperature range, it usually use to railway vehicle analysis or elevator monitoing. VI-TEX-A had equipped with high-quality front-end signal processing functions, it can ensure no signal distortion and not easily affected by the environment. It also has a high-performance calculation chip to support edge computeing and no need other extra accessories.
